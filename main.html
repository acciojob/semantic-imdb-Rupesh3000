Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. To earn his freedom, he is tasked with the seemingly impossible—planting an idea into someone's mind. This gripping story explores the nature of dreams, reality, and the power of the subconscious.
